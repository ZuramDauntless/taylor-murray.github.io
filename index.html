About Me:

My name is Taylor Murray. I am a Ph.D candidate advised by Jack Jeffries at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. Before that, I received my Bachelor's Degree in mathematics at the University of Utah. My area of research is commutative algebra; in particular, I enjoy thinking about local Cohomology and differential operators. Outside of mathematics, I love to hike, golf, play tennis, climb, and play guitar. If you look hard enough, you can find me in Avery Hall 336.
